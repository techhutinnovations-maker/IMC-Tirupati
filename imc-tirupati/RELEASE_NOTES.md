# Release Notes

## Version 1.1 - 19 July 2026

### Overview
This release focuses on improving the IMC Tirupati website experience with a more polished interface, stronger community engagement flow, and better navigation across the site. The website now includes a more structured home experience, dedicated community pages, improved forms, and a cleaner branding experience for visitors and members.

### 1. Homepage Experience
The homepage was developed as the main entry point for the community website and includes a strong visual first impression for visitors. It brings together the hero section, community introduction, featured wings, gallery highlights, testimonials, FAQ, and contact areas in a streamlined layout. The page is designed to guide users toward joining the community, exploring the wings, and contacting the team.

### 2. Navigation and Routing
The site includes a dynamic navigation system that allows users to move smoothly between the homepage and standalone pages. Navigation is managed through a central app view system, which helps switch between sections and full-page experiences without losing context. The navbar and footer provide quick access to different sections such as Home, About, Our Wings, Gallery, Contact, and Join Team.

### 3. Header and Footer
The header and footer were improved to reflect the IMC Tirupati identity more clearly. Both sections now display the imported logo and community branding text, improving recognition and consistency across the website. The footer also provides quick access to key website links, legal information, and contact details.

### 4. Hero and Landing Section
The hero section was developed to introduce the community in a bold and modern way. It uses strong typography, animated visual elements, and call-to-action buttons that encourage visitors to join the community or explore upcoming events. The hero creates an engaging first impression while aligning with the overall brand aesthetic.

### 5. About Section
The About section explains the purpose and identity of IMC Tirupati. It communicates the value of the community, the spirit behind the organization, and the role of its different wings. This section helps visitors understand the mission and encourages deeper exploration of the organization.

### 6. Wings Section
The Wings section is one of the core sections of the website. It presents multiple community wings in a visually rich and interactive layout. Users can browse different wings, see their details, and view the associated activities and curated team information. The left and right navigation buttons allow users to move between wing cards and change the displayed content dynamically.

### 7. Gallery Section
The Gallery section showcases community moments, activities, and highlights. It provides a visual representation of the culture and spirit of IMC Tirupati, helping visitors connect with the community beyond text. This section adds depth to the site and strengthens the storytelling of the brand.

### 8. Testimonials Section
The Testimonials section includes social proof and user feedback to build trust and credibility. It helps show the community’s impact from the perspective of those involved. This section is an important part of the homepage experience because it adds authenticity and engagement.

### 9. FAQ Section
The FAQ section was added to address common questions and reduce friction for visitors. It improves clarity around the community, participation, and membership-related information. This helps visitors quickly find the answers they need without requiring direct contact.

### 10. Contact Section
The Contact section was built to give users a straightforward way to reach out. It includes contact details and an enquiry experience that allows visitors to connect with the IMC team. This section complements the WhatsApp and join flow by making the community more accessible.

### 11. Join Team Page
A dedicated Join Team page was created as a more immersive community entry experience. It includes a modal-based flow with terms, details, submitting, and success states. The page is designed to guide users step by step and make the onboarding experience feel polished and structured.

### 12. Form Experience
The website includes several interactive form-like experiences for community engagement. The Join Team form collects basic personal information such as name, email, contact number, profession, and terms agreement. Validation is included to ensure incorrect or incomplete submissions are handled properly before submission.

### 13. Third-Party Integrations
The website uses third-party services to support community engagement and external communication. The most notable integrations include:
- WhatsApp integration for contact and community outreach.
- Google Apps Script endpoint for joining form submission handling.
- External event links through Linktree for community updates.
- Instagram link integration for social presence and audience engagement.

### 14. WhatsApp Integration
WhatsApp integration was added across the website so users can quickly connect with the community. This includes the floating WhatsApp button, contact call-to-action links, and redirect behavior after joining. The updated number is used consistently for the website’s direct communication flow.

### 15. Google Form / Sheet Submission Flow
The Join Team form submission is connected to a Google Apps Script endpoint so that submissions can be captured and processed externally. The current flow sends key form data such as name, email, contact, profession, source, submission time, and terms agreement to a remote endpoint. This allows the community team to collect and track new join requests.

### 16. Theme and Visual Experience
The website includes dark/light theme support and visual transitions that improve readability and modern styling. Theme switching is handled smoothly, and the interface adapts to different color modes while preserving overall design consistency.

### 17. SEO and Metadata
The application includes SEO-related updates such as page title changes, meta description handling, and canonical link updates depending on the current page or section. These updates help improve the website’s presence and structure for search engines and external sharing.

### 18. Responsive Design
The website was developed with responsive behavior in mind so that users can access the content comfortably across mobile, tablet, and desktop screens. The layout adjusts for different screen sizes, and key navigation and content sections remain usable across devices.

### 19. Accessibility and Interaction Improvements
The website includes improved interaction states such as hover effects, transitions, and clear button behavior. These details provide a more polished experience and make the site feel more alive and intentional for visitors.

### 20. Overall Release Impact
This release significantly improves the IMC Tirupati website by making it more visually complete, community-oriented, and easier to navigate. The platform now better supports visitor engagement, membership requests, and the presentation of community activities, all while maintaining a modern and polished experience.

### Notes
This version represents a major step forward in turning the website into a stronger digital home for the IMC Tirupati community, with improved presentation, interaction, and conversion flow.
